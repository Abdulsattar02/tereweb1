FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a ride?

Download the Tere app, create an account, enter your pickup and drop location, and request a ride. It's that simple!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, M-Pesa, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and our digital wallet.

Are all drivers verified and safe?

Yes! All drivers undergo background verification and are rated by our community. You can also track your ride in real-time.

What if I need to cancel my ride?

You can cancel free of charge up to 2 minutes before the ride starts. After that, a small cancellation fee applies.

How do I become a driver?

Click "Apply to Drive" below and complete the verification process. You'll need a valid license and vehicle documents.

What about my data security?

Your data is encrypted end-to-end and we comply with international data protection standards. Your privacy is our priority.
